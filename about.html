🇮🇳 The Problem

India carries the world's largest diabetes burden — and prediabetes is invisible

136 million Indians already have diabetes. Crores more are prediabetic and don't know it — prediabetes has no symptoms, so it goes undetected for years until it quietly becomes Type 2 diabetes. By then, reversal is far harder.

DiabFit exists at exactly this gap: a free, 3-minute screening that tells you your real prediabetes risk before a diagnosis ever becomes necessary.

DiabFit Team

DiabFit was built on one belief: prevention is more powerful — and far cheaper — than treatment. We are not a hospital or a pharmaceutical company. We are health advocates who think knowing your risk should cost nothing — the screening is completely free. Only the optional ₹49 personalised Activity Plan is paid, since it takes real effort to generate.

Our screening uses the WHO FINDRISC questionnaire, aligned with ICMR and IDF guidelines for South Asian populations. Our Knowledge Centre pairs that same rigor with respect for traditional Indian wellness knowledge — presented honestly, alongside modern research, never as a replacement for medical care.

⚖️ Medical Disclaimer

What DiabFit is — and what it is not

DiabFit is a risk screening tool, not a diagnostic device. It does not diagnose prediabetes or diabetes — only a doctor and a blood test (HbA1c or fasting plasma glucose) can do that. If your result shows moderate or high risk, please book a blood test and speak to a doctor.

Traditional wellness content in our Knowledge Centre is shared as historical and cultural knowledge — it is not a substitute for medical treatment, and does not claim to cure diabetes.
